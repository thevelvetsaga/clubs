bi0s is a student-led cybersecurity team and one of the top CTF teams in India. We play CTFs every weekend, and most of our learning happens by building (exploits), breaking, and discussing real challenges together.

On weekdays, we meet regularly at 3:40 PM for practice, sessions, and problem-solving, so the community stays active beyond competitions. We follow a mentorship model where seniors guide juniors, and juniors later become mentors themselves, which keeps the learning cycle going.

Every odd semester, we run a recruitment CTF to find passionate students who want to learn and contribute. No prior experience is required; curiosity, consistency, and willingness to learn matter most.

If cybersecurity sounds interesting, come check it out with us. Join the community, start learning at your own pace, and definitely drop by one of our [meetups](https://www.bi0smeetup.in/).
