Smriti - the photography and design club, is a platform for individuals interested in visual creativity and expression. 
It brings together students who are passionate about photography and design, providing opportunities to learn, collaborate, and develop their skills. 

The club organizes structured workshops, weekly challenges, and competitions that encourage consistent practice and improvement. Photowalks offer practical exposure and a chance to explore creative perspectives in real-world settings. The flagship event, Fotofiesta, highlights talent and innovation within the community. 

Smriti fosters a balanced environment that supports both creative exploration and disciplined skill development in photography and design.
