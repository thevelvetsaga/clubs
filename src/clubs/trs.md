TRS Student Chapter is a community of students and researchers brought together by a shared curiosity for robotics and intelligent systems.
We focus on building a strong foundation in robotics through collaboration, mentorship, and hands-on learning. From workshops and seminars to projects and competitions, we create spaces where students explore how machines perceive, think, and act.
More than just events, we emphasize knowledge sharing and peer learning — connecting students with experts, researchers, and like-minded builders across institutions. At our core, we’re about exploration — learning by doing, building by experimenting, and pushing the boundaries of what we can create with technology.
