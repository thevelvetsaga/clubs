We are Team Odyssey, a student robotics team from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bengaluru. We build rover end to end — mechanical, electrical, software carefully going through each of the phases.

Our work spans the full stack of a robotic system. We design and fabricate the mechanical structure, build and wire the electronics that power and connect everything and write the software that brings it all to life. Every subsystem is something we've thought through, built ourselves, and learned from along the way.
