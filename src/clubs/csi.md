CSI at Amrita Bengaluru is basically a space for people who are genuinely into tech and want to do more than just study it. We’ve got folks interested in everything—development, AI, cybersecurity, design, and a lot more.

The idea is simple: don’t just use technology, actually understand it and build things with it. We try to encourage curiosity, problem-solving, and going a bit deeper than what you usually get in classes, while still keeping things practical and relevant.

We regularly share useful resources, ideas, and trends, and also work on hands-on stuff so you’re constantly learning something new.

At the end of the day, CSI is where you grow, meet like-minded people, and push yourself to become a better technologist.
