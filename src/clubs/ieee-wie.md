**About**

Connecting, supporting, and inspiring females worldwide, and facilitating their recruitment and retention in STEM fields. IEEE WIE actively encourages participation from all individuals, regardless of gender or background.

**The WIE Mission**

To inspire women and girls worldwide in STEM fields, fostering technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

**The WIE Vision**

To be globally recognized for its contributions in broadening and inspiring participation in STEM fields.

We focus not just on technical growth, but on shaping confident individuals who can carve their own paths in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

We actively foster learning through collaboration, mentorship, and real-world exposure — ensuring every member grows both technically and professionally.

**What we do:**

- **Career Talks, Leadership Sessions & Technical Deep Dives** Featuring industry leaders and experts sharing insights on careers in AI, data science, semiconductors, and beyond — along with sessions on cloud ecosystems, AI in infrastructure, agentic AI, Fintech, and big data — complemented by inspiring career journeys that offer both real-world perspective and technical depth.
- **Panel Discussions** Conversations like “Carve Your Niche” that guide students in discovering their strengths, building unique career paths, and navigating challenges in tech.
- **Workshops & Hackathons** Hands-on sessions and competitive events focused on Generative AI and Agentic AI, where participants build, experiment, and innovate under real-world constraints.
